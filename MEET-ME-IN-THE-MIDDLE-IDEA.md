## Product Idea

Create a mobile and web app called "Meet Me in the Middle" that helps two people quickly agree on a place to meet.

## Problem

When people are in different locations, deciding where to meet is slow and awkward. The current negotiation is usually manual and unclear about fairness.

## Users and Use Cases

- Professional networking meetups
- Dating app meetups

## Core Inputs

- Each participant location
- Each participant willingness-to-travel percentage split (for example 70/30)
- Shared preference tags (for example coffee, cocktails, vintage shops)

## Core Output

- Ranked places and points of interest between both people

## What Roadmap Output Should Include

1. A phased roadmap from discovery through launch and stabilization.
2. For each phase:
   - objective
   - core deliverables
   - success metrics (leading and lagging)
   - major risks
   - assumptions
   - explicit non-goals
3. 30-day outcomes and 90-day outcomes with concrete target signals.
4. Early decisions to lock in across product, UX, data, and technical architecture.
5. A short dependency map and sequencing rationale.
6. A concise first 2 weeks execution plan.

## Constraints

- Keep the output concise and implementation-ready.
- Prioritize high-leverage MVP scope first, then expansion.
- Make tradeoffs explicit.
