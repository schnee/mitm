# How to Test the Live Site

Use this quick guide to test the live app: https://mmitm.giantmetalrooster.com

## Copy/paste invite

Test with me on Meet Me in the Middle: https://mmitm.giantmetalrooster.com
I’ll host and create a session, then share the invite link with you.
We’ll both set our location and travel preferences.
Then we’ll review the suggested spots together and pick one.

## 1) Host flow (person starting the session)

1. Open the site and choose the option to start a new meetup.
2. Enter your location (or allow location access).
3. Set your travel preference (for example, 50/50 or 70/30).
4. Copy the invite link or code and send it to your partner.
5. Wait for your partner to join, then continue to see suggested meeting places.
6. Review the suggested places and pick one together.

## 2) Invitee flow (person joining)

1. Open the invite link (or enter the invite code on the site).
2. Enter your location (or allow location access).
3. Set your travel preference.
4. Submit and wait for the shared results.
5. Review the suggested places and confirm your preferred option.

## 3) Manual coordinates format

If you enter coordinates manually, both of these formats are accepted:

- Decimal: `42.3601, -71.0589`
- HMS/DMS: `42°21'36.4"N, 71°03'32.0"W`

## 4) What feedback to report

Please share short notes on:

- Anything confusing in the steps or wording
- Any errors, slow loading, or broken screens
- Whether place suggestions felt fair for both people
- Anything that made choosing a place easier or harder
